This is where it all started...

In 1996 my father came home from his visit to America and brought this book about modern architecture. As a little girl, all I wanted to see in this book were the pictures. There were pages in it about the Fallingwater and it impressed me so much. I was mesmerised. Later I’ve learnt about Frank Lloyd Wright and completely fallen in love with the shapes he created. I absolutely love the way he composes new geometry to become part of its surroundings so seamlessly. So subtle yet striking. I’m still a big fan.
