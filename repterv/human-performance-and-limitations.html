TEM (Threat and Error Management) model
organisational threats
  • ​de-icing error of the ground crew
  • Organisational threats can be controlled (for example removed or, at least, minimised) at source by aviation organisations and are usually latent in nature.
environmental threats
  • that occur due to the environment in which flight operations take place.
  • contaminated runway
  • Some environmental threats can be planned for and some will arise spontaneously, but they all have to be managed by flight crews in real time.
