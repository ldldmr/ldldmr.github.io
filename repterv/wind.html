Geostrophic Wind
  • straight
  • parallel to isobars
  • pressure gradient force and coriolis force
  • present only higher than 15deg latitude
  • depends on density, Earth rotation, latitude
  • L at low lat H at high lat: same grad wind speed
  • at the equator no Coriolis force
  • Coriolis force deflect the wind on N hem to the right
Gradient Wind
  • curved
  • along curved isobars
  • pressure gradient force and coriolis force and centrifugal force
  • at LOW wind speed less than HIGH
